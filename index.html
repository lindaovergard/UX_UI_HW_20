Happy Hamper

Not everyone has the time or interest in doing laundry, and so it becomes a big source of stress in their daily life. They might not even have a washer and dryer at home, and going to the laundromat eats up a lot of time. But there are people out there that love doing laundry…

HappyHamper connects these two groups of people. By using the app, the user is connected with a person, also known as a HappyHamper, that will pick up your hamper, do your laundry per your preferences, and then drop it off in a timely manner.

Research

We conducted User Interviews with a wide variety of Users to gain insights into where their pain points are during the entire process of laundry, as well as what measures need to be in place for the user to feel confident in the service and leaving their laundry with a stranger.

“I thought it was literally fantastic having a laundry service do my laundry.”

“I don’t like sorting, I don’t like folding, I guess if I had to pick one thing I like it would be adding detergent.”

I would like for someone to definitely wash fold and dry my clothes, and definitely fold.

Problem Statement

Based on our research we see that the process of laundry is causing unnecessary stress for people. Our users find it difficult to keep up with their laundry.

How might we provide a method for individuals to complete their laundry in a reliable way, so they do not have to worry about a full load of laundry or having clean clothes, and can instead do as they wish with their precious time off?
